If you're in need of additional support, please consider reaching out to the following resources:

  • Mental Health New Zealand:
    mhanational.org
  • Lifeline Helpline:
    0508 828 865 or text 4357 (Available 24/7)
  • Suicide Crisis Helpline:
    0800 543 354 (Available 24/7)
  • International Helplines:
    befrienders.org
  • Therapy Resources & Directories:
    psychologytoday.com

RE:CONNECT is not a substitute for professional help. Please reach out if you are in crisis.
